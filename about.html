About US

Launched in 2024, RentMe.com, One of India's best property portals, deals with every aspect of the consumers' needs in the real estate industry. It is an online forum where buyers, sellers and brokers/agents can exchange information about real estate properties quickly, effectively, and inexpensively. At RentMe.com, you can advertise a property, search for a property, browse through properties, build your own property microsite, and keep yourself updated with the latest news and trends making headlines in the realty sector.

Why RentMe.com?

At present, RentMe.com prides itself for having around nine lakh property listings spanning across 600+ cities in India. Of all, the website held over 5.7 lakh paid listings. In addition to providing an online platform to real estate developers, brokers, and property owners for listing their property for sale, purchase, or rent, RentMe.com offers advertisement stints such as microsites, banners, home page links, and project pages to the clients for better visibility and branding in the market.

With the ever-evolving online search behavior, RentMe.com shares updated information pertinent to real estate activities, assisting prospective buyers to make informed buying decisions. We make online property search easier, quicker, and smarter!
